Modifiers are evaluated in this order.
NOPUNC - No periods, apostrophes, or commas. Frequently used with LOWCAP.
ALLCAP - Text is translated to all caps
LOWCAP - Text is translated to all lowercase
ALTCP1 - Text alternates from capital to lowercase
ALTCP2 - Text alternates from lowercase to capital
RNDCAP - Characters will be randomly capped or uncapped
TTLCAP - The first character of every word will be capitalized.
ASTRSK - Text inside prefixes will be enclosed in asterisks.
ROLPLY - Roleplay prefix. The next line should contain the prefix, which will appear before a space and then the string.
